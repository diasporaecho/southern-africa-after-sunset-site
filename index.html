Main airport strategy

Arrive in Johannesburg. Prefer departing from Cape Town if the price and schedule work. Backup: keep a Johannesburg return and fly Cape Town → Johannesburg early with a safe buffer.

Backup route

If the return flight must stay from Johannesburg: Cape Town → Johannesburg with a large same-day buffer. If Kasane → Maun logistics do not work cleanly: save Okavango for a future Botswana trip and continue from Chobe to Cape Town.

Emotional Anchor

New Year energy → Victoria Falls wonder

The trip starts with a tasteful New Year celebration in Johannesburg, then opens into the mist, rainbows, and Zambezi sunset atmosphere of Victoria Falls. For the family page, the story stays simple: a bright city start followed by one of Africa’s great natural wonders.

Botswana Safari Section

Two safari moods: Chobe + Okavango

Botswana stays family-friendly here: river wildlife, mokoro water channels, big skies, and quiet safari moments.

Chobe / Kasane

River safari and elephant country

Chobe is the high-impact wildlife stop: a sunset boat safari, a morning game drive, and golden light over the river.

Maun / Okavango

Mokoro through the Delta

The Okavango piece is quieter and more reflective: water channels, reeds, birds, and a slow day in the Delta.

Cape Town Finish Section

A golden-hour Cape finale

The finish is coastal and relaxed: beaches, views, food, culture, and a final Cape Town glow. Think Sea Point walks, Clifton or Camps Bay sunset, Table Mountain views, and a possible Winelands day.

  • Beaches and promenade walks
  • Table Mountain or Signal Hill views
  • Waterfront lights and final dinners
  • Cape Winelands as the relaxed day-trip option

Final page mood

Coastline. Wine country. Warm evening light.
